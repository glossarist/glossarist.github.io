# Adopting Glossarist

At high level, the effort necessary to adopt Glossarist can be separated into three steps.

1. [Concept Management Principles](/docs/adopt/1-workflows/) — Understand the workflows Glossarist expects
2. [Infrastructure Setup](/docs/adopt/2-infrastructure/) — Prepare your concept registry data storage
3. [Migrating Existing Data](/docs/adopt/3-migration/) — Convert your existing terms into Glossarist format
